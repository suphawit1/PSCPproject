Battle of Stickman(pygame) - Stickman figthing game

Have 2 mode play:
1.Player vs Player
2.Player vs Bot

Have 3 charactor:
1.Gunner
2.Hammer
3.Samurai

Player 1 Control
A - moveleft
D - moveright
W - jump
R,T - Attack

Plater 2 Control
Arrow to move
Numpad 1,2 - Attack

Enjoy,have fun
